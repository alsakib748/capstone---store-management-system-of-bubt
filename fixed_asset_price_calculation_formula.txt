Here's the current price and depreciation formula used in the fixed asset report:

  Formula:
  Step 1: Calculate Total Days and Remaining Days
  - Total Days = Purchase Date → Expiry Date  - Remaining Days = Today → Expiry Date

  Step 2: Calculate Expiry Ratio
  - Expiry Ratio = Remaining Days / Total Days

  Step 3: Calculate Current Price and Depreciation
  - Current Price = Original Price × Expiry Ratio
  - Depreciation = Original Price - Current Price

  Step 4: Calculate Stock Value
  - Stock Value = Current Price × Quantity

  Example:
  - Original Price: 1000 TK
  - Purchase Date: 2026-01-01
  - Expiry Date: 2026-12-31
  - Total Days: 365
  - Today: 2026-05-10
  - Remaining Days: 235

  Expiry Ratio = 235 / 365 = 0.644
  Current Price = 1000 × 0.644 = 644 TK
  Depreciation = 1000 - 644 = 356 TK
  Stock Value = 644 × Quantity
